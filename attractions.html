Shore Temple, Mahabalipuram
  • Overview: 7th‑century Pallava masterpiece facing the Bay of Bengal.
  • Tips: Best at sunrise/sunset. Pair with Pancha Rathas and Arjuna’s Penance.
  • Time needed: 1.5–3 hours
  • Nearby: cafés, local craft stores, viewpoints
Meenakshi Amman Temple, Madurai
  • Overview: Vibrant gopurams and a sprawling complex with 1,000‑pillar hall.
  • Tips: Dress modestly; certain areas are for Hindus only. Visit for evening rituals.
  • Time needed: 1.5–3 hours
  • Nearby: cafés, local craft stores, viewpoints
Brihadeeswarar Temple, Thanjavur
  • Overview: UNESCO‑listed Chola marvel built in 1010 CE.
  • Tips: Light & Sound show on select evenings. Combine with palace museum.
  • Time needed: 1.5–3 hours
  • Nearby: cafés, local craft stores, viewpoints
Ooty & Nilgiri Railway
  • Overview: Cool hill town with gardens; heritage ‘toy train’.
  • Tips: Book train seats early. Try fresh tea and homemade chocolates.
  • Time needed: 1.5–3 hours
  • Nearby: cafés, local craft stores, viewpoints
Kodaikanal Lake
  • Overview: Boating, cycling loop and viewpoints in misty hills.
  • Tips: Evenings get chilly; carry a light jacket.
  • Time needed: 1.5–3 hours
  • Nearby: cafés, local craft stores, viewpoints
Rameswaram & Pamban Bridge
  • Overview: Pilgrim town on Pamban Island with sea vistas.
  • Tips: Train ride over the bridge is scenic; check timings in advance.
  • Time needed: 1.5–3 hours
  • Nearby: cafés, local craft stores, viewpoints
Kanyakumari
  • Overview: Southern tip of India; sunrise/sunset by the Vivekananda Rock.
  • Tips: Take the ferry when seas are calm; weekdays are quieter.
  • Time needed: 1.5–3 hours
  • Nearby: cafés, local craft stores, viewpoints
Chettinad Mansions, Karaikudi
  • Overview: Ornate homes of trading families; unique cuisine.
  • Tips: Hire a local guide for history; try ‘kuzhi paniyaram’.
  • Time needed: 1.5–3 hours
  • Nearby: cafés, local craft stores, viewpoints
