Quick Tips

  • Best time October–March for most plains; April–June for hill stations.
  • Dress code Modest attire for temples; footwear left outside.
  • Language Tamil is primary; English widely understood in cities.
  • Transport Trains/buses connect major cities; autos & app cabs for local travel.
Chennai–Mahabalipuram 3-Day Itinerary
  1. Day 1: Sunrise at Marina Beach, Fort St. George museum, filter coffee at Mylapore, evening at Kapaleeshwarar Temple.
  2. Day 2: Drive to Mahabalipuram. Arjuna’s Penance, Pancha Rathas, Shore Temple sunset.
  3. Day 3: Croc Bank stop, DakshinaChitra living museum, seafood on ECR, drive back.
