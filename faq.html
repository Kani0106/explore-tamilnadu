Frequently Asked Questions

Practical travel info for a smooth trip.

October to March is pleasant for most cities; April–June is ideal for Ooty/Kodaikanal. Monsoon (Oct–Dec on the coast) can bring heavy showers.

Trains and government/volvo buses connect cities. In town, autos and app cabs are common; ask for meter or agree the fare beforehand.

Dress modestly, leave footwear outside, keep phones on silent. Photography may be restricted in sanctum areas.

Tamil is widely spoken; English and Hindi are understood in tourist areas. Learning a few Tamil phrases is appreciated!

UPI and cards are accepted in cities; keep cash for small shops. Mobile data is reliable in most places, patchy in remote hills.
